Frequent Habits: Aesthetics meet deep analytics—100% local.

Often, you have to choose between a gorgeous design hidden behind expensive subscriptions or privacy-friendly apps that feel basic. Frequent Habits bridges this gap.

This app offers a beautiful, ultra-modern dark mode design and a depth of comprehensive statistics you will hardly find elsewhere—all completely offline on your device.

Key Features:
- Unique, Aesthetic Design: A stunning interface with vibrant accent colors, fluid animations, and a clean UI for a premium experience.
- Unrivaled Statistics: Understand your behavior through yearly heatmaps, weekday frequencies, and visually striking progress bars.
- 100% Private & Offline: No tracking, no ads, no mandatory cloud. Includes a secure, local backup feature.
- Motivation through Gamification: Build streaks, earn experience, and unlock exclusive trophies and milestones.
- Built-in Timer: Complete time-based habits (like meditation or reading) with a dedicated timer directly in the app.
- Integrated Mini-Notebook: Jot down your thoughts or reasons for a slip-up on specific days to track your patterns.

Frequent Habits gives you the most powerful tools and the most beautiful design to turn goals into real habits.
